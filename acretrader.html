AcreTrader

Brief

Create a set of social media advertisements that will showcase the benefits and ease of investing in farmland via their platform to traditional investors.

Direction

For the set of concept advertisements, the contractor I subcontract for asked that half the designs feature sourced illustrations and the other half feature sourced photography. Each would need to feature the brand’s logo and one of the seven messages provided.

Extension

After I finished the 1080x1080 advertisement designs, the client requested that each advertisement in 1200x628 for use on LinkedIn and Google.
