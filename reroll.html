Reroll

Reroll is a second-hand dice brand focused on reducing waste in the gaming community by creating cohesive dice sets from mismatch dice bins at game shops.

Brief

Develop the packaging and brand identity for a new product. The finalized concept should utilize extensive research of your audience and the trends within that community.

Background

Most gaming stores sell accessories and supplies for dungeons and dragons and other role-playing games but have noticeably wasteful packaging.
