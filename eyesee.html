Eye See

Eye See is a spiral-bound book exploring topics in vision for Western Washington University’s print production class. Each spread was written and designed by a member of our ten person team within the visual design guidelines we developed as a group.

Brief

Create a book exploring a selected topic, and produce 11 copies using a variety of print production techniques.

Concept

My design featured information about the Snellen Eye Chart and a CO2 laser-cut pocket with two inserts printed on transparencies. By placing the inserts in front of the right-hand page, the image created will blur the chart and text as a way to imitate different levels of visual acuity.
