Eye See

A spiral book exploring topics in sight and vision.

Brief

In collaboration with nine other designers, create a book exploring a topic of choice, and produce 11 copies using a variety of print production techniques. Each designer will design and develop content for a two-page spread within the book.

Concept

As a group, we decided to explore topics in vision. For the individual spread, I researched the Snellen Eye Chart and created a design explaining how the chart is used to test visual acuity.
