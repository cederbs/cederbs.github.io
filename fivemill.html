Client Dashboard

Design the client analytics dashboard UI for the Five Mill's client, Quicken.

Five Mill is a digital marketing agency based out of San Francisco. One of the many services they offer to their clients is tracking and reporting via a customized dashboard suited to the particular client's interests and needs.

A large portion of the project focused on tweaking the data visualization to fit the client's branding and maintaining a straightforward but comprehensive range of data for Quicken to utilize at a glance.
