Cosmic Brownies

Packaging Redesign for Little Debbie's Cosmic Brownies.

Brief

Redesign the packaging for an existing food item using that can be found in a convenience or grocery store. Designs will be targeted at a specific audience determined by the designer and must utilize paper as the primary packaging material.

Concept

An individually packaged version that utilizes the bright colors of the chocolate chip candies inside to create a space-inspired design for the existing product line.

Ideation

Initially I considered both bulk and individual packaging ideas, eventually settling on an individual packaging as a way to bring in new customers who may not have tried the product before.

Prototyping

I built small prototypes to test both bulk and individually wrapped packages before selecting my final concept. I tested out printing both text and patterns on vellum but ultimately decided to design a paper label to increase readability, as well as ease of scanning at the cash register.

Label

Throughout packaging the product, I tested out different colors and layouts. Initially the nutrition information was created for the packaging, but due to the size of the label and other factors, we determined that the product could be exempt from the requirement, and instead house the nutritional information line for the product.
